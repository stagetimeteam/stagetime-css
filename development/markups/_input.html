.input--placeholder-is-chosen

There is a problem with default <select> tag. It doesn't work with place placeholder attribute. It make sense at some point: at least one value should always be selected. On the other hand it looks out of place inside of a form with other inputs that actually have placeholders. So lets fake it:

Here we assign one of the options as placeholder and when it's selected we add .input--placeholder-is-chosen class. Please do the same.
