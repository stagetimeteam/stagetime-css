Attachments

At the moment there are few components that can be attached to the post:
.image-preview
.video-card (in .video-card--entry modification)
.article-card
.parsed-link

On the side of .post component we have special element that will correctly place them inside of a post: .post__attachment. This is sort of a slot for those attachable components.
