.simple-parallax

This is alternative to .parallax and it is simpler but limited. Simpler because it doesn't cut original image. Limited by two things:

1) This can only be used for images on top of the page because image starts to parallax when window's top edge reaches photo. You can see in this demo first few pixels of scrolling doesn't have any effect on parallax. It lets us to not to cut image on top for parallaxing.

2) Direction of scroll is inverted and as a result it looks like image wants to stay on the page as long as possible. It lets us to not to cut image on the bottom.

Fortunately this is exactly what we need on profile page.
