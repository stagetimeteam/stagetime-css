Innovation, creativity, and academic freedom are hallmarks of our world-class contributions in research and the arts. As one of the most comprehensive and acclaimed institutions for the study of music, the IU Jacobs School of Music plays a key role in educating performers, scholars, and music educators who influence music performance and education around the globe.
